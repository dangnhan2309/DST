Here are the pros and cons of the three PDF text extraction libraries:

PyPDF2:

Pros: Easy to use, good for extracting text from simple PDFs with minimal formatting.
Cons: Struggles with complex layouts, scanned documents, and images.
pdfplumber:

Pros: Handles more complex layouts, extracts tables, and offers better control over text positioning.
Cons: Slower than PyPDF2 and may require more memory.
pdfminer:

Pros: Accurate for extracting detailed text and layout information, handles complex PDFs well.
Cons: More complex to use, slower compared to other tools.
Choose based on your document's complexity and extraction needs.
